Kyoto, Japan


It's known for its rich cultural heritage, including ancient temples, traditional tea houses, and stunning gardens. Attractions include Fushimi Inari Shrine, Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Kinkaku-ji Temple, Gion District, and Nijo Castle. Must-try foods: sushi, ramen, and matcha desserts.
